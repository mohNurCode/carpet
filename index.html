✦ Frequently Asked ✦

What types of rugs do you offer?

Traditional Ethiopian, Contemporary, Organic Wool, Silk, Bamboo, and Custom-sized rugs.

How do I choose the right size?

Consider your room layout. For living rooms, choose a rug large enough to fit all furniture legs.

What materials are used?

Natural fibers like Ethiopian wool, organic cotton, bamboo silk, and plant-based dyes.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes! We ship worldwide with secure packaging.
