Woven by Generations, Treasured Worldwide

Yegarnure (የገርኑረ) means “of the homeland.” Our carpets are born in family cooperatives across Addis Ababa, Bahir Dar, and Harar. Using local sheep wool, madder root, and indigo, every rug takes weeks of patient hand-knotting.

Owning a Yegarnure carpet means preserving an ancient Ethiopian craft and supporting 100+ artisan families with dignity, fair wages, and cultural pride.
